\begin{abstract}
\noindent We present an experimental mapping of the Variational Quantum Eigensolver to classical optical hardware by encoding qubits in photonic polarization and spatial modes. Using wave plates, beam splitters, and dove prisms we realize a UCCSD-inspired ansatz for H$_2$ via Jones-matrix decompositions and read out energies through Stokes-parameter measurements. This approach offers an accessible photonic testbed for studying variational algorithms and prototyping optical circuit implementations.
\end{abstract}
